Founder's Message

As a former rider and driver, I founded Birdy to address the frustrations I experienced firsthand in the rideshare industry. After over 520 trips, I saw how little rideshare drivers often earn, despite being the heart of the service. Unfair pay can lead to dissatisfied drivers, which impacts safety and service quality.

At Birdy, our mission is to create a fairer system—offering savings for riders and better earnings for drivers. When drivers are valued, everyone benefits, because without them, ridesharing doesn’t exist.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is Birdy different from Uber or Lyft?

Birdy offers 10-15% lower fares, pays drivers 80-90% of fares, and focuses on community-driven service with no algorithmic punishments. We prioritize transparency, fairness, and local connections.

What are the benefits for drivers?

Drivers enjoy freedom from harsh penalties, full fare and destination transparency, the ability to set availability zones, referral bonuses, and perks like discounts on maintenance and fuel.

What perks do riders get?

Riders can choose preferred drivers, access specialized rides (e.g., for kids or seniors), earn rewards, and benefit from loyalty subscriptions with discounts and priority booking.

How does Birdy support the community?

Birdy operates as a cooperative, using profits to fund community programs and offer discounted rides for low-income riders, ensuring a fair and ethical ride-share experience.
