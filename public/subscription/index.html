From Our Heart to Yours

Birdy was built for one reason — you.
This isn’t just an app. It’s your listing platform, your clients, and your community. We simply provide the tools to help people find you — and we’re here to support you.

We know how hard you work. You’re not just drivers — you’re professionals, providers, and people with families, dreams, and hustle. We’re not here to take advantage of that. We’re here to help amplify it.

To give riders a reliable way to find and connect with you, we use tools and tech that come with real costs. But no driver should ever be left out because of money.

If you’re going through a tough time, just email us. We’ll gladly cover your subscription until you’re back on your feet. No questions asked. We can’t build Birdy without you — and honestly, we wouldn’t want to.

With gratitude, The Birdy Team
